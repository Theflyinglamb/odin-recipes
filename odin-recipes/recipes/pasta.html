Homemade Italian Pasta

Description

Here, we're going to be going over how to make homemade Italian pasta.

Ingredients

  1. Raw Noodles
  2. Pasta Sauce
  3. Onion
  4. Basil Leaves
  5. Olive Oil
  6. Lime

Steps

  1. Boil water
    While the water is heating up, go ahead and start chopping your onions. You can dice them, or cut them into slivers.
  2. Once the water is boiling, go ahead and put the noodles in and set a timer.
  3. Now, take your chopped onions and sautee them in your olive oil. You want to bring the flavor out and turn them to a dark gold shade before adding the sauce.
  4. Once the onions are ready, go ahead and add the sauce. You'll want to chop the basil leaves up and add them to the sauce as well.
  5. Once the noodles are ready, take the lime and squeeze some of the juice into the sauce.
    Now all you have to do is sauce the noodles and you're done!
